What is activate.uhc.com?

activate.uhc.com is the official activation portal that allows UnitedHealthcare members to activate and manage their health insurance accounts online.

After enrolling in a UnitedHealthcare plan, members must activate their online account to access digital healthcare tools.


Once activated, users can view plan details, download ID cards, track claims, and search in-network doctors.

How to Activate Your Account

Follow these simple steps to complete activation.

  1. Visit activate.uhc.com
  2. Enter your Member ID
  3. Verify your personal information
  4. Create login credentials
  5. Complete activation
